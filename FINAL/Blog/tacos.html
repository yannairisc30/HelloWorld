Heading to

September, 4, 2023

Location:

Website: Chinelos 2

Rating:

Nestled in Harlem, Chinelos 2 is a Mexican gem that stole my taste buds with each delightful bite. My taco adventure featured three distinct varieties, a testament to the diverse options on their menu. As a small business, the warmth and passion from the staff and owners infused each dish with love. Beyond the tantalizing tacos, Chinelos 2 offers a plethora of choices, including burritos and enticing entrees. In my experience, there's no downside to this culinary haven—just an earnest invitation to support this delightful small business!
