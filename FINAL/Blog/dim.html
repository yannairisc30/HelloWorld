Heading to House of Joy

Some Date

Location: Some Location

Website: House of Joy

Rating:

Situated in the heart of Chinatown, House of Joy offered me a second delightful experience with Dim Sum, and it quickly became my favorite. The array of options was impressive, creating a feast for the senses. My go-to dish is always the perfect combination of pork buns, while the cucumber salad stood out for its refreshing and flavorful appeal. One notable advantage of House of Joy is its affordability, allowing you to savor a generous spread without breaking the bank. However, the popularity of this spot comes with a minor drawback—be prepared for wait times that may exceed 30
