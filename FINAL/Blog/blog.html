Heading to BCD Tofu House

March, 16, 2023

Location: 5W. 32nd Street, New York, NY 10001

Website: BCD Tofu House

Rating:

BCD Tofu House, nestled in the heart of KTown (Korea Town) in downtown Manhattan, stands as a delightful Korean culinary haven. The culinary experience it offers is nothing short of phenomenal. Upon being seated, patrons are treated to a delightful array of mini side dishes, including macaroni & cheese, kimchi, cucumbers, and more. A standout dish featured in the picture is Topokki, a delectable combination of rice cakes, fish cakes, two fried dumplings, and a boiled egg bathed in a tantalizingly spicy sauce. This flavorful delight finds its place in the Appetizer section of their menu or can be enjoyed as a shareable option. What sets BCD Tofu House apart is its diverse menu, featuring a range of options, including many that cater to vegetarian preferences. A particularly commendable aspect is the attentive service—mini side dishes are promptly replenished upon request, adding an extra touch to the dining experience.

Heading to Peachy Keens

September, 23, 2023

Location: 325 W 44th St, New York, NY 10036

Website: Peachy Keens

Rating:

Peachy Keens, an American restaurant, immerses diners in a lively 80's-themed ambiance adorned with vibrant colors, creating a nostalgic and energetic atmosphere. Featured on their dinner menu is the dish in the picute that includes mac&cheese, BBQ ribs, cornbread, and coleslaw. this delightful dish stands out among various tempting options. As I enjoyed my second visit to Peachy Keens, the experience was elevated by fantastic service and a lively musical backdrop provided by a DJ—a delightful surprise! My first visit to this place was at brunch, where I indulged in their scrumptious pancakes. One of the restaurant's standout features is the impeccable service that caters to your every need, enhancing the overall dining experience. However, it's worth noting that the menu selection, albeit enticing, is somewhat limited—a minor downside to an otherwise enjoyable culinary adventure at Peachy Keens
