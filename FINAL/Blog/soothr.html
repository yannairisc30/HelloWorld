Heading to Soothr

July, 16, 2023

Location:204 E 13th St, New York, NY 10003

Website: Soothr

Rating:

Soothr truly impressed! The dining experience was exceptional, especially when it came to the delicious offerings captured in this image. In focus are two standout dishes: the Seafood Tom Yum Noodles and the Mango Sundae. The Seafood Tom Yum Noodles, a highlight from SOOTHR's Signature Noodles section, presented a delightful combination of spicy, lightly creamy, and savory Tom Yum broth. Thin rice noodles, fishcakes, squids, shrimps, and culantro added depth to this flavorful creation. Turning attention to the Mango Sundae, a delightful dessert featuring sweet sticky rice, mango compote, sweet coconut cream, and homemade mango gelato garnished with tuile. Both dishes were a symphony of flavors, leaving a lasting impression. Noteworthy was their Ice Thai Tea, a perfect complement to the dining experience. The service was efficient and attentive, although the restaurant's cozy size could pose seating challenges. Despite this, Soothr delivered a memorable and delectable dining
