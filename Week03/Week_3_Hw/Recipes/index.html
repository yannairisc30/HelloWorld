French Toast!

Background Story

French Toast origin is beleived to come from the Roman Empire it first was created just by soaking the bread
into the milk and eggs mixture. Then frying it in a pan with butter.
It came to America a few years.

Instructions

  1. Obtain a large bowl possibly one that is deep
  2. Crack 2 eggs into the bowl
  3. Pour the cup of milk
  4. Whisk or mix all the ingredients in the bowl
  5. pour the vanilla into the bowl after whisking
  6. pour the brown sugar
  7. pour desired amount of cinnamon
  8. whisk all the inggredients together
  9. Grab desired bread and put in it the mixture
  10. flip the bread in the mixture
  11. put butter in a skillet
  12. add the bread to the skillet let it cook for 2/3 minutes on each side
  13. Serve with other ingredients(like eggs, bacon, fruits, etc..) or just enjoy the french toast!
